A community space for students, alumni, and staff to share open-source projects, tools, and resources connected to Strathclyde.

## About Strathclyde

The University of Strathclyde is a public research university in Glasgow, Scotland. Founded in **1796** as the Andersonian Institute (from the bequest of John Anderson, who championed *"useful learning"*), it is Glasgow's second-oldest university. It received its royal charter in **1964** as the **UK's first technological university**, formed from the merger of the Royal College of Science and Technology and the Scottish College of Commerce.
